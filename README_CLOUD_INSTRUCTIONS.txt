HOW TO MAKE GUPTA CLOTH HOUSE LIVE 24/7 (LAPTOP OFF)
==================================================

Follow these 3 EASY steps (Takes 2 minutes):

STEP 1: Create an Account
-------------------------
Go to https://render.com and sign up for a FREE account.

STEP 2: Upload Your Shop
------------------------
1. Go to https://github.com and create a free account.
2. Create a "New Repository" called "gupta-shop".
3. Drag and drop all the files from the 'shop' folder on your Desktop into the GitHub page and click "Commit".

STEP 3: Connect to Render
-------------------------
1. On Render.com, click "New +" and select "Web Service".
2. Connect your GitHub account and select "gupta-shop".
3. Click "Deploy Web Service".

THAT'S IT! 
Your shop will now have a SMALL, EASY LINK like:
https://gupta-cloth-house.onrender.com

WANT IT EVEN SMALLER?
---------------------
Once you have your link, go to https://bit.ly or https://tinyurl.com and put your long link there. You can name it:
bit.ly/guptashop
